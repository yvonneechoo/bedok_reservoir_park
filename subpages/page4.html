Food & Beverage

Wawawa

WaWaWa is a full-service restaurant/café located in the sculpture garden in Bedok Reservoir Park, Singapore. The restaurant features a full menu of moderately priced “comfort” food influenced by German cooking traditions. The café section features a coffeehouse with a dessert bar, magazines, and space for live performers.
